Naval Fate.

Usage:
  naval_fate.py ship new [-f...] <name>...
  naval_fate.py ship <name> move <x> <y> [--speed=<kn>]
  naval_fate.py ship shoot <x> <y>
  naval_fate.py ping...
  naval_fate.py dock ARGS...
  naval_fate.py sing [-t=<title>...]
  naval_fate.py mine (set|remove) <x> <y> [--moored|--drifting]
  naval_fate.py -h | --help
  naval_fate.py --version

Options:
  -h --help            Show this screen.
  --version            Show version.
  --speed=<kn>, -s     Speed in knots [default: 10].
  -f                   Number of flags to adorn the ship with.
  -t, --title=<title>  Title of the shanty to sing,
                       specify multiple times to sing more songs
  --moored             Moored (anchored) mine.
  --drifting           Drifting mine.
